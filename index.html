About

Prodigy Of Chemistry (POC) is an online educational platform in Bangladesh focused on HSC (class 11-12) or equivalent education. We are mainly based on our facebook public group (https://www.facebook.com/groups/prodigyofchemistry) with over 80K active members/students. Interactive contents of lectures are being provided regularly on different subjects by a group of enthusiastic university students from various renowned universities around the country. Also, digital interactive live classes are being taken.

HSC Students from various cities & villages now have free access to quality educations as we highly assure, giving our best to provide the best to our dear students.

Our main goal is to provide fully quality education to each & every HSC Students out there in every corner of Bangladesh. That is why we frequently launch some private batches in every couple of months, either free or paid.

Prodigy Of Chemistry (POC) is the first & only online educational platform in Bangladesh, to provide full lengthed video lectures with Digital Interactive PDF on complete Organic Chemistry for free. Yes, we are providing full lengthed 50 lectures on complete Organic Chemistry on Youtube for free!! Students will master HSC Organic Chemistry full, after watching & understanding these 50 lectures by Muhammad Ishtiaq (founder, POC). This is why, now a days we are regular on youtube also.

So, what are you waiting for? Looking for HSC e-learning? then POC is the best online platform to sign up/subscribe.
